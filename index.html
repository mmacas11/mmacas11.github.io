Biography

I am a software/data engineer with roughly seven years of experience. I have designed different data models, Artificial Intelligence (AI) architectures, as well as web applications for solving problems in the financial, academic, and cybersecurity areas. Recently, I have been researching how AI can enhance Information Security and I have published several papers at international conferences. I have excellent written and oral communication skills in English. I am proactive and I can work in teams with people from different cultures
